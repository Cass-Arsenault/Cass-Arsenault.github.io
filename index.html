About Me

I am Cassandra Arsenault, mathematics teacher and technology enthusiast. I first discovered that I enjoyed watching others learn when I tutored my younger sister, who had a learning disability. I rejoiced with her when she had success, and supported her when she struggled. Through tutoring her and my classmates I learned that a Growth Mindset and a resilient attitude are key when trying to learn mathematics. Fostering a passion to help others learn new things and discover new opportunities for growth which flourishes to this day.

Work Areas

Teaching

My personal philosophy of teaching is to inspire an active classroom. I use group work, discussion based activities and projects to promote an investigative environment. As a result, I foster a growth mindset and a passion for lifelong learning.

Tutoring

Tutoring a student one-on-one or in a small group is a great opportunity to get to know the student, build a relationship and witness how they learn first hand. Through supporting and challenging my students, I endeavor to help them to become confident mathematicians and learners.

Technology

Technology is constantly changing how we learn, work and communicate. Modern professionals need to understand how to best utilize technology to make tasks simpler and more efficient. Through my experiences as technical support and teaching in a laptop school, I know that having up to date knowledge on various technologies is vital to working in a professional environment.
