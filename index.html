About Me

I am Cassandra Arsenault, mathematics teacher and technology enthusiast. From a young age I enjoyed mathematics and the world of numbers. Mathematics seemed so applicable to daily life, since it is all about trying to find good solutions to problems. I discovered that I enjoyed watching others learn when I tutored my younger sister, who had a learning disability. I rejoiced with her when she had success, and supported her when she struggled.

Through tutoring her and my classmates I learned that a Growth Mindset and a resilient attitude are key when trying to learn mathematics. Fostering a passion to help others learn new things and discover new opportunities for growth which flourishes to this day.
